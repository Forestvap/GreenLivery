Services

We have services that benefit both drivers and car owners.

Taxi-Cab Management

We manage inactive taxis for car owners. Whether you need to go on vacation or have some other reason for not driving your car, you don't have to worry about it being a liability anymore. As long as you have your car, it can earn you money.

Car Rental

We rent taxi-cabs to licensed drivers that need a car to drive. Stop letting your Hack license go to waste. Not having a car is no longer a reason to not make money. We can help you get into a street hail livery or Uber car today.

How It Works

Driving with Green Livery is as simple as these three steps.

1. Register

Sign up on the website and submit all of your information to the Green Livery team.

2. Lease a car or get it managed

If you're a car owner, let Green Livery maintain and lease your car to licensed drivers. If you're a driver, lease cars by the week from Green Livery to earn extra cash with minimal commitment.

3. Earn cash!

Collect a bank roll weekly!

About

The evolution of Green Livery and its team.

  • 2013-2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Green Livery started out as a simple idea. The CEO Sergio Sanchez began Green Livery as an experiment managing taxis for a couple of friends. They were so impressed with Sergio's work that they encouraged him to officially start the business and became Green Livery's very first investors!

  • January 2014

    An Agency is Born

    Green Livery official set up its headquarters in the Bronx, NY and hit the streets to find it's very first clients! From a few clients to dozens, our awesome company just keeps growing!

  • May 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    To keep up with Green Livery's excelerating growth, CEO Sanchez declares a company move to Astoria Queens, the heart of the taxi industry!

  • Semptember 2015

    Phase Two Expansion

    With a great team and the best clients, we're ready for additional investors and the next phase of our growth!
